Privacy Policy · Last updated: April 2026

TailorMeasure is a tool for tailors to manage client measurements and garment patterns. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

What we collect

  • Phone number — used only for login via SMS OTP. We do not share it with any third party.
  • Client data — names, phone numbers, and body measurements that you enter. This data belongs to you and is stored securely in your account.
  • Garment patterns — the patterns and formulas you create are stored in your account and are not accessible to other users.

How we use your data

  • To authenticate you via Firebase Phone Auth (Google).
  • To store and retrieve your patterns and client measurements.
  • We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

Data storage

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore). Each tailor's data is isolated — no other user can access your clients or patterns. Data is secured by Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules.

Third-party services

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database.

Your rights

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. You own all the data you enter into TailorMeasure.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please reach out via the app's GitHub repository.
