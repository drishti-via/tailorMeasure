What is TailorMeasure?

TailorMeasure is a web application designed for professional tailors. It allows tailors to securely log in with their phone number, define garment patterns with custom measurement formulas, store client body measurements, and instantly compute the exact cutting and stitching dimensions needed for each garment. The app supports both Indian and Western garment styles and is available in English and Hindi.
